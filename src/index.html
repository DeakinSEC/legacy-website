Deakin Software Engineering Club

The Deakin Software Engineering club is home to students from many backgrounds interested in software engineering, information systems, technology and associated disciplines. The DSEC community is about both fun and learning, with members collaborating on anything from coding problems to practical projects. DSEC coordinates with industry to represent the interests of members.
